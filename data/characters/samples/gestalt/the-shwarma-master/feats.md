### Weapon Focus (Scimitar)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Combat |
| **Source** | Core Rulebook |
| **Prerequisites** | Proficiency with selected weapon, base attack bonus +1 |

**Benefit**: You gain a +1 bonus on all attack rolls you make using the selected weapon.

*Free from the Kensai archetype. The foundation. Doubled by [Spell Perfection](#spell-perfection) on [Shocking Grasp](spells.md#shocking-grasp), providing +2 to the Spellstrike attack roll when delivering that specific spell.*

### Intensified Spell

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Metamagic |
| **Source** | Advanced Player's Guide |
| **Prerequisites** | None |

**Benefit**: An intensified spell increases the maximum number of damage dice by 5 levels. You must actually have sufficient caster levels to surpass the normal maximum to benefit from this feat. No other variables of the spell are affected, and spells that inflict damage that is not modified by caster level are not affected by this feat. An intensified spell uses up a spell slot one level higher than the spell's actual level.

**Interactions**:
- Shocking Grasp normal cap: 5d6 (at CL 5+)
- Intensified cap: 10d6 (at CL 10+)
- Yusuf's CL: 16 — reaches full 10d6 cap
- With [Magical Lineage](features.md#magical-lineage) + [Wayang Spellhunter](features.md#wayang-spellhunter) stacking on Shocking Grasp: the +1 level cost is reduced back to 0. **Intensified Shocking Grasp remains a 1st-level slot.**

*The most important feat in the build for Yusuf's primary damage spell. Without Intensified, Shocking Grasp would cap at 5d6 (avg 17.5) and be mediocre at level 16. With it, he's dealing 10d6 (avg 35) per Spellstrike, before metamagic multipliers, from a 1st-level slot.*

### Power Attack

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Combat |
| **Source** | Core Rulebook |
| **Prerequisites** | Str 13, base attack bonus +1 |

**Benefit**: You can choose to take a –1 penalty on all melee attack rolls and combat maneuver checks to gain a +2 bonus on all melee damage rolls. This bonus to damage is increased by half (+50%) if you are making an attack with a two-handed weapon, a one-handed weapon using two hands, or a primary natural weapon that adds 1-1/2 times your Strength modifier on damage rolls. This bonus to damage is halved (–50%) if you are making an attack with an off-hand weapon or secondary natural weapon.

When your base attack bonus reaches +4, and every 4 points thereafter, the penalty increases by –1 and the bonus to damage increases by +2.

**At BAB +12**: **−4 attack / +8 damage** (one-handed use with Aisha).

*Power Attack is mandatory on any full BAB-ish martial. The +8 damage per iterative is the difference between "scimitar does weapon damage" and "scimitar does weapon damage that actually matters next to Shocking Grasp." The -4 attack penalty hurts, but with Yusuf's base +22 to hit, he's still landing against anything short of CR-20 titans.*

### Spell Penetration

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | General |
| **Source** | Core Rulebook |
| **Prerequisites** | None |

**Benefit**: You get a +2 bonus on caster level checks (1d20 + caster level) made to overcome a creature's spell resistance.

**Effective**: d20 + 16 (CL) + 2 = **d20 + 18** vs SR.

*Prerequisite for [Greater Spell Penetration](#greater-spell-penetration), and doubled by [Spell Perfection](#spell-perfection) on Shocking Grasp. Worth taking on any caster who will face SR-equipped enemies (outsiders, dragons, late-game monsters), which at level 16 means basically everyone.*

### Empower Spell

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Metamagic |
| **Source** | Core Rulebook |
| **Prerequisites** | None |

**Benefit**: All variable, numeric effects of an empowered spell are increased by half. An empowered spell deals half again as much damage as normal, cures half again as many hit points, affects half again as many targets, and so forth, as appropriate. An empowered spell uses up a spell slot two levels higher than the spell's actual level.

**Interactions with trait-stacked Shocking Grasp**:
- Intensified + Empowered SG: normally 4th-level slot
- With [Magical Lineage](features.md#magical-lineage) + [Wayang Spellhunter](features.md#wayang-spellhunter): **2nd-level slot**
- Damage: 10d6 × 1.5 = avg 52.5 per cast

*Metamagic feat #2 on the path to [Spell Perfection](#spell-perfection). Also useful on its own — Empowered Intensified SG from a 2nd-level slot is tremendous value per slot. With [Empowered Magic](features.md#empowered-magic) arcana (if taken later) you could Empower for free once per day.*

### Combat Reflexes

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Combat |
| **Source** | Core Rulebook |
| **Prerequisites** | None |

**Benefit**: You may make a number of additional attacks of opportunity per round equal to your Dexterity bonus. With this feat, you may also make attacks of opportunity while flat-footed.

**Effective**: +4 AoOs/round from DEX mod. Stacks with [Superior Reflexes](features.md#superior-reflexes) (10 AoOs from INT).

**Total AoOs per round**: 1 (base) + 4 (DEX from Combat Reflexes) + 10 (INT from Superior Reflexes) = technically the two abilities work differently. Per the Kensai archetype text: "This bonus stacks with the bonus from Combat Reflexes." Final effective count: **14 AoOs per round**.

Also enables AoOs while flat-footed (though [Iaijutsu](features.md#iaijutsu) already does this with Yusuf's chosen weapon).

*Combat Reflexes is the cheap enabler that multiplies Superior Reflexes. Paired with [Counterstrike](features.md#counterstrike), Yusuf punishes virtually every enemy that moves in his threat zone. Enemy wizards casting defensively are the most common victims.*

### Quicken Spell

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Metamagic |
| **Source** | Core Rulebook |
| **Prerequisites** | None |

**Benefit**: Casting a quickened spell is a swift action. You can perform another action, even casting another spell, in the same round as you cast a quickened spell. A spell whose casting time is more than 1 round or 1 full-round action cannot be quickened. A quickened spell uses up a spell slot four levels higher than the spell's actual level. Casting a quickened spell doesn't provoke an attack of opportunity.

You can only cast one quickened spell per round.

**Interactions with trait-stacked Shocking Grasp**:
- Quickened Intensified SG: normally 6th-level slot
- With traits: **4th-level slot**
- Combined with [Spell Perfection](#spell-perfection) (free metamagic on SG): Quickened Intensified SG becomes a **2nd-level slot** (starts at 1st with traits applied, SP makes the Quicken free)

**This is the combo that enables two Shocking Grasps per round**:
1. Swift action: Quickened SG → delivered as Spellstrike (1st free attack with weapon)
2. Full-round action: [Spell Combat](features.md#spell-combat) full attack + cast SG → delivered as Spellstrike

Both spells deal full 10d6 through scimitar at 15-20 crit range.

*Metamagic feat #3 on the path to Spell Perfection. Without Quicken, there's no way to cast two spells in one round outside of True Magus (level 20). With it, Yusuf's alpha strike doubles overnight.*

### Dimensional Agility

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | General |
| **Source** | Ultimate Combat |
| **Prerequisites** | Ability to use the abundant step class feature or cast *dimension door* |

**Benefit**: After using *abundant step* or casting *dimension door*, you can take any additional actions you have remaining in that turn. You also gain a +4 bonus on concentration checks made for teleportation spells.

*Prerequisite for [Dimensional Assault](#dimensional-assault). Without this, casting Dimension Door ends your turn — you cannot attack or cast after teleporting. With it, teleportation becomes a movement option, not a turn-ender. Yusuf qualifies at character level 7 (Wizard 7, 4th-level spells available).*

### Dimensional Assault

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | General |
| **Source** | Ultimate Combat |
| **Prerequisites** | Dimensional Agility |

**Benefit**: As a full-round action, you can use *abundant step* or *dimension door* as a charge. You must teleport to the closest space from which you can attack the enemy, and you must be able to enter that space. You can teleport up to your normal movement speed, but you cannot bring other creatures with you when you use this ability. You otherwise follow the normal rules for charging.

*Second step in the Dimensional Dervish chain. Useful on its own for single-attack charges at unusual ranges, but the real payoff is [Dimensional Dervish](#dimensional-dervish).*

### Improved Critical (Scimitar)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Combat |
| **Source** | Core Rulebook |
| **Prerequisites** | Proficient with weapon, base attack bonus +8 |

**Benefit**: When using the weapon you selected, your threat range is doubled.

**Scimitar normal threat**: 18-20/×2
**With Improved Critical**: **15-20/×2** (30% threat chance)

Does not stack with *keen* weapon property.

*With a 30% threat range and Yusuf's +36 to confirmation rolls (BAB +12, WF +1, STR +4, weapon +5, Critical Perfection +10, Critical Focus +4), he is confirming crits on essentially every threat. This feat, combined with [Spellstrike](features.md#spellstrike)'s ×2 spell damage on crits, turns a normal Shocking Grasp hit (10d6) into a crit (20d6). Over 5 attacks per round, expect 1-2 crits at 30% rate = average +35-70 damage per round just from this feat.*

*Taken as a Wizard bonus feat at level 10 (Wizard bonus feats can be metamagic, item creation, or Spell Mastery — but this is taken as a Magus bonus feat slot instead, freeing the Wizard bonus feat for metamagic). See sheet.md feat table for exact slot assignment.*

### Dimensional Dervish

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | General |
| **Source** | Ultimate Combat |
| **Prerequisites** | Dimensional Agility, Dimensional Assault, base attack bonus +6 |

**Benefit**: As a full-round action, you can use *abundant step* or *dimension door* to make a full attack. The attacks can be made at any point during the movement allowed by the ability, and you can break up your movement by teleporting multiple times during the attack. Each teleport must cover at least 5 feet, and the total distance teleported cannot exceed the normal distance allowed by the ability.

**Effective at level 16**: Cast Dimension Door, full attack, teleporting up to 1,120 ft total (CL 16 × 70 ft) distributed before, between, and after each attack. Minimum 5 ft per jump.

*This is the mobility capstone. Combined with [Spell Combat](features.md#spell-combat), Yusuf can Spell-Combat Dimension Door + full attack + teleport between each swing. Useful for:*

- *Hitting multiple scattered enemies in one full attack*
- *Breaking line of sight between attacks (ally dies? Teleport to them, hit the killer on the way)*
- *Escaping a grapple mid-fight without sacrificing a turn*
- *Approaching a flying enemy*

*The 3-feat tax is heavy, but at level 16 Yusuf has the feat slots to spare thanks to gestalt's doubled bonus feat progression (Magus + Wizard + Human + character feats).*

### Critical Focus

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Combat |
| **Source** | Core Rulebook |
| **Prerequisites** | Base attack bonus +9 |

**Benefit**: You receive a +4 circumstance bonus on attack rolls made to confirm critical hits.

*Qualifies via [Critical Perfection](features.md#critical-perfection) — Yusuf uses his magus level as his fighter level for critical feat prerequisites, so he meets BAB +9 at magus level 9. Stacks with [Critical Perfection](features.md#critical-perfection)'s +10 for a total +14 to crit confirmation from these two sources alone.*

*Mainly a prerequisite for critical feats (Bleeding Critical, Staggering Critical, Stunning Critical) if Yusuf wants to expand the crit tree. At level 16 in a single class he doesn't have the feat slots, but the prereq is already met for any future retraining.*

### Greater Spell Penetration

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | General |
| **Source** | Core Rulebook |
| **Prerequisites** | Spell Penetration |

**Benefit**: You get a +2 bonus on caster level checks (1d20 + caster level) made to overcome a creature's spell resistance. This bonus stacks with the one from Spell Penetration.

**Effective**: d20 + 16 (CL) + 2 (SP) + 2 (GSP) = **d20 + 20** vs SR.

**With [Spell Perfection](#spell-perfection) on Shocking Grasp**: Both Spell Penetration AND Greater Spell Penetration are doubled, giving +4+4 = **+8** bonus on top of CL 16, for **d20 + 24** vs SR on Shocking Grasp specifically.

*At +24 vs SR, Yusuf bypasses the spell resistance of essentially every creature in the Bestiary at CR 16. CR 20 creatures typically have SR 30-31, which he still beats on a 6+ — reliable.*

### Spell Perfection

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | General |
| **Source** | Advanced Player's Guide |
| **Prerequisites** | Spellcraft 15 ranks, at least three metamagic feats |

**Benefit**: Pick one spell which you have the ability to cast. Whenever you cast that spell you may apply any one metamagic feat you have to that spell without affecting its level or casting time, as long as the total modified level of the spell does not use a spell slot above 9th level. In addition, if you have other feats which provide a bonus or take away a penalty to the spell's save DC, caster level check, or the spell's damage, those bonuses and penalties are doubled when applied to this spell.

**Chosen Spell**: [Shocking Grasp](spells.md#shocking-grasp)

**Metamagic feats available for free application**: [Intensified](#intensified-spell), [Empower](#empower-spell), [Quicken](#quicken-spell), [Maximize](#maximize-spell).

**Doubled feat bonuses on Shocking Grasp**:
- [Weapon Focus (Scimitar)](#weapon-focus-scimitar): +1 → **+2** to Spellstrike attack roll when delivering SG
- [Spell Penetration](#spell-penetration): +2 → **+4** vs SR
- [Greater Spell Penetration](#greater-spell-penetration): +2 → **+4** vs SR
- Combined SR bypass for SG: **CL +8** vs SR

**Typical use**: Apply Quicken as the free metamagic. Cast Quickened Intensified SG as a swift action from a 1st-level slot (base 1st + Intensified 0 via traits + Quicken 0 via Spell Perfection). Then use the standard action / full-round Spell Combat to cast a *second* Shocking Grasp.

**Alternate use**: Apply Maximize as the free metamagic. Cast Maximized Intensified SG from a 1st-level slot for guaranteed 60 electricity damage — no roll needed, no variance.

*Spell Perfection is the single most powerful caster feat in the game for a build that focuses on one key spell. Yusuf's entire damage engine runs through Shocking Grasp; Spell Perfection makes that engine twice as efficient and twice as accurate at the same time.*

### Maximize Spell

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Metamagic |
| **Source** | Core Rulebook |
| **Prerequisites** | None |

**Benefit**: All variable, numeric effects of a spell modified by this feat are maximized. Saving throws and opposed rolls are not affected, nor are spells without random variables. A maximized spell deals maximum damage, cures the maximum number of hit points, affects the maximum number of targets, etc., as appropriate. A maximized spell uses up a spell slot three levels higher than the spell's actual level.

**Interactions with trait-stacked Shocking Grasp**:
- Maximized Intensified SG: normally 5th-level slot
- With [Magical Lineage](features.md#magical-lineage) + [Wayang Spellhunter](features.md#wayang-spellhunter): **3rd-level slot**
- Damage: 10d6 maximized = flat **60 electricity damage**, no variance

**With [Spell Perfection](#spell-perfection)**: Maximize applied for free to Shocking Grasp, costing only a 1st-level slot. **Every Spellstrike deals guaranteed 60 electricity damage**, plus weapon damage, plus crit doubling on a 15-20 (which would make it 120 on a crit — the spell portion doubles even though the weapon uses its own multiplier).

*The metamagic feat #4 and the potential target of Spell Perfection. Yusuf can choose Quicken OR Maximize as his Spell Perfection application each time he casts Shocking Grasp — Quicken for volume, Maximize for consistency. On boss fights where landing damage matters more than throwing more dice, Maximize is the correct choice. On swarm rounds, Quicken doubles your spell attacks.*

*Taken as the Wizard bonus feat at level 15. Wizard bonus feats must be metamagic, item creation, or Spell Mastery — Maximize qualifies as metamagic.*
