## Build Decisions

### Why Pure Spell Sage 10?

Investigated **Arcane Savant** and **Pathfinder Savant** prestige classes as potential synergies for cross-list casting. Both offer permanent access to off-list spells but require feat taxes (Skill Focus), lose wizard bonus feats, and at level 10 would reduce Focused Spells to 1/day (losing the L8 upgrade). The Spell Sage archetype already does what the concept wants via Spell Study — prestige classing would trade raw wizard power for marginal gains in an area the archetype already covers. Pure Wizard 10 maximizes:
- 5th-level spells at CL 10
- 2/day Focused Spells (+4 CL)
- 2/day Spell Study
- Full wizard bonus feat progression
- 24 spell slots/day

### Why These Feats?

The feat selection follows three themes:

1. **Crafting (Scribe Scroll → Craft Wondrous Item → Craft Wand)**: McGyver builds things. Three item creation feats let him produce scrolls, wondrous items, and wands — the party's entire consumable and magic item supply during downtime. Craft Wondrous Item alone effectively doubles WBL on crafted items.

2. **Conjuration focus (Spell Focus → Augment Summoning)**: Conjuration is the broadest, most versatile school — summoning, teleportation, creation (pits, tentacles, clouds, walls), and healing subschool. +1 DC on the most-used control spells, plus meaningful summoned creatures.

3. **Flexibility (Fast Study, Heighten Spell, Spell Penetration)**: Fast Study is the keystone — 1-minute spell swaps turns the spellbook into a toolbox. Heighten Spell adds DC flexibility. Spell Penetration addresses the mid-level SR problem. No wasted feats on hyper-specialized combos.

### Why Favored Class Bonus → Spells?

Human wizard alternative FCB adds one spell from the wizard list to the spellbook per level (at least one level below max castable). Over 10 levels, that's 10 free spells — roughly 3,000+ gp saved in scribing costs. For a wizard whose identity is "I have a spell for everything," a fat spellbook is more valuable than 10 HP. With 62 HP, Mage Armor, Shield, and Mirror Image, McGyver's survivability is adequate.

### Spell Selection Philosophy

The prepared loadout covers the wizard's core jobs: battlefield control (Grease, Glitterdust, Create Pit, Web, Stinking Cloud, Black Tentacles), buffs (Mage Armor, Shield, Haste, Greater Invisibility), mobility (Fly, Dimension Door, Teleport, Overland Flight), and utility (Dispel Magic, See Invisibility, Arcane Eye, Wall of Stone). The one damage spell (Fireball) is included because sometimes you just need a fireball.

The "borrowed" spells via Spell Study showcase what makes McGyver unique: healing (Remove Disease), restoration (Restoration), raising the dead (Raise Dead), morale buffing (Good Hope), and condition immunity (Freedom of Movement, Death Ward). These are problems that normally require a cleric — McGyver can handle them all, at the cost of time and spell slots.

With Fast Study, 2-3 spell slots are left open each morning and filled on-the-fly as situations arise. This turns the character from "I prepared the wrong spells" to "give me 60 seconds."
