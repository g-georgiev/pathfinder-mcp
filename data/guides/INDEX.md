## Coverage Summary

**Classes with dedicated guides (19):** Alchemist, Arcane Trickster, Arcanist, Barbarian (x3), Bard (x3), Cleric, Druid (x2), Fighter, Gunslinger, Inquisitor, Investigator, Magus (x2), Monk (x3), Oracle, Paladin (x2), Rogue, Sorcerer, Witch (x2)

**Classes without dedicated guides:** Antipaladin, Bloodrager, Brawler, Cavalier, Hunter, Kineticist, Medium, Mesmerist, Occultist, Psychic, Ranger, Shaman, Shifter, Skald, Slayer, Spiritualist, Summoner, Swashbuckler, Vigilante, Warpriest, Wizard (standalone)

The gestalt guide covers combo ratings for most missing classes. Check [Zenith Games' Guide to the Guides](https://zenithgames.blogspot.com/2012/11/the-comprehensive-pathfinder-guides.html) for more community guides.
