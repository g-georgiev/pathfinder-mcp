Example Builds
* TEAMWORK MAKES THE DREAM WORK: Halfling Sister-In-Arms Cavalier 5/Bard 1/Battle Herald X
   * Just all of the teamwork bonuses.
* CHARGE!!!: Phalanx Soldier Fighter 3/ Daring Champion Cavalier 8 / Mammoth Rider 1
   * Order of The Sword
      * FEATS BY LEVEL
      * Level 1: Mounted Combat (Fighter), Nature’s Soul
      * Level 2: Ride By Attack (Fighter)
      * Level 3: Spirited Charge
      * Level 5: Animal Ally
      * Level 7: Power Attack or Boon Companion
   * Now you can wield a lance as a one handed weapon...which means you can precise strike.
   * Challenge + Precise Strike + Mounted Mastery + Spirited Charge = INSANE charge damage
      * 2(Your level) + Your Strength + Your Mount’s Strength
      * Also your mount is massive.
   * Nature’s Soul + Animal Ally + Boon Companion gets you fully leveld Companion
   * Mounted Combat + Ride By Attack + Spirited Charge can be picked up via fighter levels to keep it from being TOO feat starved.
* Scroll Seargent: Ironbound Order of the Tome Samurai 3 / Child of Amazen and Acvana Fighter 5/ Cyphermage 1/ Scrollmaster Wizard 1?
* On God: Ironbound Samurai 3 / Base Fighter 5 / Ironbound X
   * Spend ALL fighter feats on Unconquerable Resolve.
   * Enjoy getting a slightly silly amount of temp hp.
