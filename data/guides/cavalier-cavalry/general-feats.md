# General Feats

General Feats
* (4/5) Practiced Tactician: More tactician! WOO! We love this ability so we love this feat!
* (5/5) {CAV} Horse Master:  Use your character level for your mount instead of your class level! Great for multicassers, useless for anyone else. If you can take this, take it!
* (5/5) Chain Challenge: Do you like to CHALLENGE your MAIN MECHANIC and don’t feel like taking Order of the Flame? Take this. Honestly you should always take this because you don’t get a lot of challenge and should be ripping people to shreds. Especially good for Daring Champions and Ironbound Swords.
* (6/5) Leadership: Our first 6/5 feat. Why? Because for a feat at level 7, you get ANOTHER CHARACTER SHEET. This is far and away the strongest feat in all of pathfinder...and it barely has any prerequisites. If your DM allows this, it’s objectively the best option.
* (3/5) Improved Initiative: Going first is amazing! It’s a pretty solid bonus.
