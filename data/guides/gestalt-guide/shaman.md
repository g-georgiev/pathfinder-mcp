# Shaman Combinations

Shaman


Shamans, like druids, have a back row caster list. Unlike druids they don't substitute shapeshifting. There's a hex, but the duration is much shorter and the progression is a lot slower. If you want a divine blaster/controller/SoDer they're your class, but if you want a front liner or archer their spell list is lousy and their hexes mostly inapplicable. 


General recommendation for gestalt: caster build, ideally pairing with another Wis-based full caster with a complementary spell list, but some partial caster combinations also work well (Wis-based or not). Should pair fairly well with Monk or work OK with Fighter as a reach build. Try to get yourself at least one of the Fort or Ref save from the second class.
