# Spiritualist Combinations

Spiritualist


The spiritualist is another pet class, with a phantom about halfway in power between an animal companion and an eidolon. As such it's usually more useful in gestalt games with a smaller party size. However the phantom does have useful tricks outside being a meatless meat shield, such as delivering touch spells at range, buff and debuff auras, and incorporeal scouting. The bonded manifestation can also be used to enhance the spiritualist's own abilities. Two archetypes to consider. First, the Fractured Soul switches the spiritualist from a wisdom-based to a charisma-based class, improving stat synergy for some matches. Second, the Ectoplasmaticist swaps out the phantom entirely for the ability to manifest ectoplasmic weapons, which are essentially a longsword or pair of shortswords that gain reach and magical enhancement as you level. You also gain a limited form of spellcombat/spellstrike, allowing you to cast a spiritualist spell during a full attack. These abilities allow nice battlefield control and action economy for many martial and “reach caster” builds. Interestingly, the spiritualist can wear armour but can also gain armour bonuses from bonded manifestation or the ectoplasmatist, so it works well with both armoured and unarmoured classes. 


Or you can be a boring person and go with cleric with the phantom of a champion of your deity. Better armor, nine level spells, wis focus. Or Druid if your DM allows you to have a Phantom and an Animal Companion at the same time. Gunslinger can work with you shooting and a Jealousy Phantom (your dead lover that you want to avenge, maybe?) protecting you in the frontlines.  


Spiritualist // Summoner
Basics: Med BAB, d8, Fort and Will, 4 skills, two 6-level casting, Wis Cha


Spiritualist // Swashbuckler (Fractured Mind & Ectoplasmatist)
Basics: Full BAB, d10, all saves, 4 skills, 6-level casting, Dex Cha Wis
Oddly enough the Ectoplasmatist makes one of the best gestalt swashbucklers. The lash can be a one-handed slashing weapon, and thus a valid choice for Slashing Grace. You can pick up Combat Reflexes at level 3 or 5 when your weapon develops reach and control the battlefield like a whip swashbuckler but with better damage and fewer feat requirements. With Fractured Mind your stats are simple, and you get all good saves and some support casting. 


Spiritualist // Warpriest
Basics: Med BAB, d8, Fort and Will, 4 skills, two 6-level casting, physical Wis
You should be able to use the Ectoplasmatist's lash as a Sacred Weapon.
