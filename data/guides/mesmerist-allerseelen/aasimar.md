# Aasimar

Aasimar


        Aasimar are also a great all-arounder race for mesmerists—you can think of them like humans, but with no bonus feat and better stats, darkvision, and energy resistances. If you stick with the variant heritages that I’ve outlined below, you have almost no chance of screwing one up, but there’s also nothing specialized about aasimar that really make them stand out from the pack.


Pros
	Cons
	Huge variety of variant heritages. Agathion-, angel-, and azata-blooded aasimar will give you the best results; each beats out the base aasimar attribute arrays pretty handily.
	None, they’re just not quite as inspiring in some areas (notably favored class bonuses) as other races.
	Two stat bonuses, no stat penalty. That should speak for itself! Angel- and azata-blooded aasimar can give you stat boosts to both your most important physical stat and your Charisma with no penalty—tasty.
	

	Human favored class bonus, with the scion of humanity racial trait. This lets you get +2 to your towering ego bonus, but being humanoid does have its drawbacks, as well.
	

	Good resistances. Call me crazy: I like getting resistances as racial traits. Resist 5 might not seem like much, but it’ll save your bacon more than you might give it credit for.
	

	Darkvision.
