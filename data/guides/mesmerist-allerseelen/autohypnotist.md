# Autohypnotist

Autohypnotist


The Basics


        A mesmerist archetype that debuffs itself in exchange for slightly more powerful hypnotic stare penalties. Awful.


        Versatility: -2
        Power: -2


        Stacks With: 


The Abilities


        (1) Autohypnosis (Su): Alters hypnotic stare. Ha! Wow, this archetype has some nerve. Someone at Paizo was like, “Hey, what if we made mesmerists an archetype where they had to debuff themselves?” and then everyone sat around and had a big laugh. To clarify: no, no, a thousand times no, you do not want to apply your hypnotic stare and bold stare debuffs to yourself, even if it means you debuff your enemy slightly more.


        (5) Wide Stare (Su): Replaces mental potency. Wide stare won’t even do anything when you get it, since your hypnotic stare penalty doesn’t increase to -3 until 8th level. Even when it does, there’s a decent chance that you’ll be debuffing allies as well as enemies, and that’s simply not worth it.


Is It Worth It?


        Not if it were the only archetype available to the mesmerist class, no.
