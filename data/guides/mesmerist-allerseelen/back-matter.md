Back Matter


        If memory serves, Follow My Voice marks my 6th class guide in the Pathfinder 1e space, and man, let me tell you, the job gets better all the time. I don’t know if you’d noticed lately, but life out there is pretty rough right now. Through it all, though, we’ve got each other, we’ve got this fun make-believe game where we get to go for spins in the Imagination Chopper, and we’ve got Golarion, constantly getting into trouble so that we can get it back out again. I’ve gotten emails, Reddit messages, and Patreon support from so many of you at this point, and I’m awed by your kindness, grace, and generosity in the midst of a worldwide pandemic, civil unrest, and all the private griefs and hardships that we all carry around with us all the time. It’s an honor to write for you and with you. I still look back at the wide-eyed kid I felt like I was when I started getting serious about this hobby back in 2017, and I hope that this guide (and all the other content I’ve written) has helped it all feel a little less intimidating. If you’re new, this guide is dedicated to you—because I was new once, too.
        If you’re looking for ways to get more involved with me, you’re always free to drop me a line on Reddit, Twitter (@AllSoulsGaming1), or Patreon, where you can support my ongoing guide-writing efforts and catch me writing a piece of third-party content once in a blue moon. (And I do mean once in a blue, blue moon.) You’ll probably see me back again in a few months with an updated version of my very first guide, The Inquisitor’s Symposium, or perhaps with the vigilante class guide I have in the works; or heck, it might be the guide to Style feats I have in production, or finishing rating all 3039 spells in Pathfinder 1e so that you crazy loons can keep on doing crazy things with create pit and mad monkeys. You’ve thought about it. Don’t lie. Regardless of where we see each other again, just remember that it’s better to adventure with friends. You’ve got a hug from me coming if we ever meet in real life, but until then, may all your swords be sharp, and all your rolls Natural 20s.


All my best,
Chris (/u/Allerseelen)
