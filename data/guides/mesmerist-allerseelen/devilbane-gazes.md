# Devilbane Gazes

Devilbane Gazes


Devilbane Impediment: More concentration check debuffs. I think I’d prefer Distracted, but Impediment also isn’t terrible.
Devilbane Mundanity: Better at penetrating spell resistance, but come on, given the option between Sapped Magic and Mundanity, which is better? Sapped Magic, every time.


Devilbane Dampening: Eh, even with the -3 bonus at 8th level, and even against outsiders for double effect, you’re still only able to eliminate the equivalent of energy resistance 5. Can that really compete with Psychic Inception, Disorientation, and Sapped Magic?
Devilbane Dismissal: Reducing the duration of summoning spells by half is still likely to give casters enough time to either kill you or get killed themselves.
Devilbane Withering: For very specific outsider-heavy campaigns like Wrath of the Righteous or Hell’s Rebels, Withering could easily achieve a blue rating. It’s super narrow, though—a wasted bold stare if you’re fighting literally any other type of enemy.


Devilbane Binding: You don’t even get planar binding on your spell list. Why would you want this? Maybe a cohort helping out a diabolist prestige class, or something?
