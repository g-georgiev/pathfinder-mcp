# Elf

Elf


        Elves aren’t terrible mesmerists, but they’re not phenoms, either. Especially since half-elves can poach nearly all of the strong portions of the elven racial chassis with few of the weaknesses, it doesn’t make a lot of sense to me to recommend elves to you as a mesmerist class. You’re welcome to do so if that’s the character concept you have, of course, but my job is to point out what is mechanically optimal. In this instance, half-elf beats elf, hands down.


Pros
	Cons
	Elven magic and weapon proficiencies. Elves get some bonuses to penetrate spell resistance, as well as a number of useful weapon proficiencies. Both are useful to mesmerists.
	No bonuses to Charisma, and a penalty to Constitution. Hmmm. This isn’t the worst thing in the world, but it’s also not the best. Mesmerists are weak against Fortitude saves, and at least some mesmerist builds live in melee, where HP counts. It’s hard to justify this when half-elves get nearly everything that elves do, but with more options.
	Low-light vision, but no darkvision.
	

	Good favored class bonus for swashbuckler-esque builds. If you’re looking to boost an acrobatic feint build, look no further than the elven favored class bonus. (The one from Occult Adventures, that is—the Horror Adventures elven favored class bonus is terrible.) Highly recommended for vexing daredevil builds.
