# Feints

Feints


Mesmerizing Feint / Greater: I suppose I can understand thematically why feints don’t work well against mindless creatures—I guess they’re just too dumb to react to sucker punches like humanoids?—but even so, two feats just to be able to feint the full range of targets in the Pathfinder bestiaries seems like an unnecessary burden to place on a combat style that’s already not, like, ludicrously strong. Fortunately, only vexing daredevils will really get into hardcore feinting, and they get these feats for free.
