# Gaslighter

Gaslighter


The Basics


        An evil-aligned mesmerist who torments foes with their own reflections in the mirror.


        Versatility: -1
        Power: +0


        Stacks With: 


The Abilities


        (1) Alignment: I’d expect nothing less, given the name of the archetype. Proceed.


        (1) Consummate Cruelty: Replaces consummate liar. Probably an upgrade in both cases, feats and skills. Intimidate isn’t necessarily better than Bluff, but the fact that you get the bonus to all Intimidate checks—not just those to demoralize—increases its utility. Very easy to build a demoralize build here. Moreover, dirty trick maneuvers can be better than feinting, since the blinded option of dirty trick also denies enemies their Dexterity to AC.


        (3) Horrid Mask (Su): Replaces touch treatment (minor) and alters painful stare. Way too finicky. I prefer touch treatment.


        (6) Phantasmal Force (Su): Replaces touch treatment (moderate). Not much to get excited about here, either. Phantasmal force could easily backfire and make an enemy double down on trying to kill you.


        (10) Phantasmal Foes (Su): Replaces touch treatment (greater). Ah, at last, something decent. A more or less permanent +2 for melee allies to hit the target you designate, not to mention reliably proc’ing sneak attack damage. If you had to give up touch treatment, this isn’t a bad replacement.


        (14) Corrosion of Sanity (Su): Replaces touch treatment (break enchantment). Phantasmal force would count for the purposes of this ability, but for the most part you’ll be relying on spell slots to do this kind of work. The debuff is nice, but when you pit it against spells like mind fog (which grants a -10 penalty to Will saves) you start to realize how underpowered corrosion of sanity is for its level.


Is It Worth It?


        It’s slightly more powerful on offense than the base mesmerist, but I’m not entirely convinced that surrendering touch treatment is a good idea, especially when the replacements are fairly mediocre.
