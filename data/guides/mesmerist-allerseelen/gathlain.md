# Gathlain

Gathlain


        Gathlain are primarily distinguished from other classes by their fly speed, which is a neat trick that most other races can’t pull off. Having just come off the almost meth-like high of the gathlain favored class bonus for kineticists, my finger is itching to rate these guys purple, but they’re a pretty average mesmerist class. You won’t go wrong with them, but you also won’t any more right than with gnomes or halflings.


Pros
	Cons
