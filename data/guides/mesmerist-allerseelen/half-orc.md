# Half-Orc

Half-Orc


        Oof, but it’s difficult to pick between half-orcs and half-elves! Sacred tattoo + Fate’s Favored is an enormous boost to the mesmerist’s survivability that easily competes with the half-elf’s adaptability trait. The half-orc’s big advantage is that it’s the best damage dealer out of the core races, thanks to its phenomenal favored class bonus. Painful stare is the mesmerist’s primary source of damage, remember!


Pros
	Cons
	Floating stat bonus. Floating stat bonuses are the name of the game for half-elves, half-orcs, and humans, and they’re flexible enough to let you build any kind of mesmerist you want.
	None. I’m getting tired of saying it, but many of the core races are great as mesmerists. Except you, dwarves. You know what you did.
	Incredible favored class bonus. Adding half a point of damage to painful stare may seem like a small thing, but I can’t emphasize enough that it’s not! It adds on about 30% more damage to each painful stare you trigger with your own attacks, and 100% more damage if an ally triggers painful stare. If you want to deal optimal damage as a mesmerist, you need to be a half-orc, plain and simple.
	

	Great racial traits. Sacred tattoo combined with the Fate’s Favored trait is a classic combo to give you +2 to every save at 1st level, and while it’s a little cliche by now, it’s ruthlessly effective. You can trade away weapon familiarity, too—single-class mesmerists won’t have the Strength scores to take advantage of the racial weapons, and multiclass mesmerists will have picked up other weapon proficiencies from their dip. You get to pick what it’s replaced with.
	

	Darkvision.
