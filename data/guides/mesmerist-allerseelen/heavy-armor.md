# Heavy Armor

Heavy Armor


Clockwork Armor (CL 11, 33,350 gp): Love the Strength and Dexterity bonuses you get while in this puppy. Easily a top-shelf late-game heavy armor.
Honor’s Panoply (CL 5, 12,550 gp): Some debuffing, true, but mostly big sacred bonuses to your saves against many of the enemies you’ll fight in most Paizo APs.
O-Yoroi of Imperial Rule (CL 13, 68,400 gp): DR/—, force damage, blasting...good stuff.
Prismatic Plate (CL 13, 160,650 gp): I mean, sure, if you can afford it, why not?
Suit of Inverted Jade (CL 14, 25,000 gp): Big profane bonuses to saves vs. a wide variety of effects that are dangerous both to you as a psychic casting class and to you as a Fortitude-weak class.
