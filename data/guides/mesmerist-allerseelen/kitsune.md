# Kitsune

Kitsune


        Unlike so many of the selected other races, which have not much to distinguish them from the pack of perfectly competent core races gunning for the mesmerist class, kitsune do have something: excellent spellcasting, especially in the arena of enchantment spells. Between their racial enchantment bonuses and the Magical Tail feats opening up new avenues of enchantment, kitsune make monumentally strong spellcasters. If you really want to lean all the way into spellcasting, grab the hate-monger archetype and trade away those mesmerist tricks for more spells known!


Pros
	Cons
	Excellent stat bonuses. Dexterity and Charisma are aces, as usual, and a penalty to Strength isn’t devastating.
	None.
	Superior casters of enchantment spells. Kitsune get a racial +1 to the save DC of any enchantment spells they cast. Want to take a stab in the dark about which class gets a huge number of enchantment spells on their spell list?
	

	Two good favored class bonuses. You can take extra mesmerist tricks—tried and true—or else branch out into the Magical Tail feats. Doing so would be an incredible way to build a spellcasting mesmerist.
	

	Low-light vision, but no racial darkvision.
