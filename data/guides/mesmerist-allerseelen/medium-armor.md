# Medium Armor

Medium Armor


Celestial Plate Armor (CL 8, 28,650 gp): As in the light armor category, the celestial series wins again. Great AC bonuses, accommodates buffing from cat’s grace or similar effects, and grants a 1/day fly effect.


Grim Gloom Mail (CL 3, 2,380 gp): The real attraction of grim gloom mail is the ability to add even more debuffing to an enemy’s saves. Against tough targets, it’s just one more layer of destruction. The protection against good ability obviously won’t come into play that often in mainstream Paizo APs.
Lucky Drunk’s Mail (CL 3, 4,840 gp): The medium armor equivalent of the calamitous mail, the lucky drunk’s mail also gives you a luck bonus to all your saves that can be doubled with Fate’s Favored. Consider wisely.
Tiger’s Hide (CL 10, 54,115 gp): Great AC bonuses, pounce during the opening round of combat, and DR 5/— vs. natural attacks. Not much more you could ask for.


Numerian Resistance Plate (CL 6, 16,350 gp): A fun anticaster armor that can really mess up necromancy or evocation specialists.
