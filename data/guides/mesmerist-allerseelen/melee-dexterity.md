# Melee (Dexterity)

Melee (Dexterity)


   * Swashbuckler 1 / UnRogue 3: A swashbuckler 1 dip is every bit as good for Dexterity-based melee mesmerists as an inspired blade swashbuckler 1 dip is for Dexterity-based melee investigators. You get a pool of panache that you can use with parry and riposte, free martial weapon proficiency, Weapon Finesse, and a modest BAB boost. Great for kickstarting Dexterity builds. Unchained rogue 3 is also a valid option for getting Dexterity to damage, along with some sneak attack, evasion, trapfinding, and a rogue talent. Rake is a nice rogue archetype if you want to do more demoralizing and less trapfinding.


Ranged (Dexterity)


   * Musketeer Swashbuckler 1 / Holy Gun Paladin 2 / Mysterious Stranger Gunslinger 1: A...mesmerist with a gun? Sounds weird, but it can work. Guns require a bunch of feats for reloading in addition to the normal ranged combat feats, though, so take that for what it’s worth.
   * Divine Hunter Paladin 2 / Arrow Champion Swashbuckler 1 / Bolt Ace Gunslinger 1: Probably one of the better options for a bow-wielding mesmerist.
