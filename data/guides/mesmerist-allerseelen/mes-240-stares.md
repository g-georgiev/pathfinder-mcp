# MES 240: Stares

MES 240: Stares


Before we get started on our bold stares, it’s worth recapping what the strengths and weaknesses of the class feature are. As far as strengths go, well, these are some incredibly strong debuffs, rivaled perhaps only by witch hexes, and they don’t require enemies to fail saves or you to pass spell resistance penetration checks—if you stare at someone, they’re debuffed. As far as weaknesses go, first and foremost bold stares are mind-affecting, so they cannot affect mindless creatures (vermin, e.g.) or other creatures that are immune to mind-affecting effects (constructs and undead, e.g.). Second, bold stares have a very short range, 30 feet, and automatically end of the target of your stare moves out of that radius. Third, bold stares always affect only one creature—no AoE debuffing for you unless you do it with spells. Alright! Let’s get to the ratings.
