# MES 660: Magic Items

MES 660: Magic Items


If I’m being honest, there aren’t a great many good items for mesmerists—psychic classes in general, really. Since they were fairly late additions to the Pathfinder canon, the repertoire of cool, class-specific items simply never built up for them in the same way that it did for core classes. If I were building a mesmerist, I’d devote myself first to the Big Six and a mesmerizing tattoo (obviously), snag a metamagic rod or two, grab a few items for social interaction, and then spend the rest on consumables—wands, scrolls, and potions never go out of style for spontaneous spellcasters, who need versatility more than anything else. Maybe mesmerists will come earlier in the production cycle if they ever make it to Pathfinder 2e, and we can hope for some better items. If you’re not satisfied with what you see here, a brief reminder that I wrote a massive guide for Pathfinder 1e’s wondrous items and unique armor/weapons called The Armamentarium, which should point you in the right direction; /u/SorteKanin also has The Collection, which is a more user-friendly search engine experience.
