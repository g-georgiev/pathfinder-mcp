# MES 801: Sample Builds

MES 801: Sample Builds


        Finally, what guide would be complete without a few sample builds to help out the newer players among us? Long-time readers may notice that this section includes quite a bit more multiclassed builds than I usually create; in this instance, I can’t tell whether that’s because the mesmerist is so starved for feat options and armor/weapon proficiencies, or whether it’s because there are so few worthwhile mesmerist archetypes, or whether it’s because of something else entirely. At any rate, I feel obliged to remind you that I do not consider myself a Pathfinder powergamer by any stretch, and while all of these builds are well-optimized, they’re not intended to let you single-handedly wipe the floor with encounters, enslave demon lords, generate infinite money, or any of the other shenanigans you see in theorycrafted builds. They’re intended to help build you a mesmerist who can hold their own in combat and out of it, and contribute one or more useful tools to the party that you happen to be adventuring with. Alright, go have some fun!
