# Nagaji

Nagaji


        Nagaji are descendents of the various races of naga, and like their serpentine ancestors, nagaji maintain some of the hypnotic qualities of snakes about them. For nagaji mesmerists, this fact is reflected in their favored class bonus, which allows them to use spells like hypnotic pattern on much higher-level enemies than they’d normally be able to. Nagaji aren’t necessarily better than any other race at spellcasting, but they make for hardy frontline fighters with great stat distributions for Strength builds and racial bonuses to saves and AC.


Pros
	Cons
	Ideal stat distribution. Ideal for Strength builds, that is: +2 Strength and Charisma with a penalty to Intelligence is everything that a mesmerist could want.
	None. Nagaji could make interesting and effective enchanters or illusionists—a nice contrast with gnomes and kitsune.
	Good racial save bonuses. Bonuses against poisons help you out with your weak Fortitude save, and bonuses against mind-affecting effects help you maintain the emotion components of your spells.
	

	Interesting favored class bonus. I’ll give Paizo this: the nagaji favored class bonus is unique. Adding more HD on top of your mental potency class feature gives you the potential to be able to use effects like cause fear or color spray well past their normal expiration dates. Enemies will still outstrip the HD limits faster than you can raise your max HD, but give Paizo credit for trying.
	

	Low-light vision, but no racial darkvision.
