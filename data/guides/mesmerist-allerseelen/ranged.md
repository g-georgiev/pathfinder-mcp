# Ranged

Ranged


Clustered Shots: A classic for avoiding DR on ranged builds.
Manyshot: Mesmerists don’t benefit hugely from extra attacks with Rapid Shot, since their ability to trigger painful stare is based off of the number of Manifold Stare feats they have, but Manyshot is just extra damage on the top. Always good.
Point-Blank Shot / Precise Shot / Improved Precise Shot: The three most essential ranged feats for mesmerists. If you’re playing with Elephant in the Room feat tax rules, just skip Point-Blank Shot—what you really want is Precise Shot.


Deadly Aim: As a ¾-BAB class without many meaningful attack bonuses, mesmerists aren’t ideally situated to use Deadly Aim, but it’s not a horrible option, either.
Rapid Shot: Since most of a mesmerist’s damage will come from painful stare, which is limited in its number of uses per round by Manifold Stare, getting extra attacks doesn’t help as much as it would in other classes. Nice to be able to saturate your painful stare triggers, though.


Rapid Reload / Crossbow Mastery: Mesmerists emphatically do not have the feats for crossbow builds. You’re better off multiclassing or using racial traits for longbow access.
