# Thought Eater

Thought Eater


The Basics


        A mesmerist who specializes in impersonating others.


        Versatility: -1
        Power: -2


        Stacks With: 


The Abilities


        (1) Assume Identity (Su): Replaces consummate liar. Sure, I’ll bite. Getting the ability to disguise yourself this effectively is a lot of fun, and could help you if you’re going deep undercover somewhere. Less combat-effective than consummate liar, of course, but to my mind more flavorful.


        (2) Assume Morality (Su): Replaces towering ego. I haven’t yet met a class feature that was truly worthy to take towering ego’s place, and assume morality isn’t breaking that streak. Sure, it’ll help you pass yourself off as another creature, but there are plenty of spells that could have done something similar without giving away your biggest Will save bonus.


        (3) Assume Knowledge (Su): Replaces touch treatment. Wow, all instances of touch treatment, in exchange for some bonus skill ranks? No, absolutely not worth it.


        (20) Consume Identity (Su): Replaces rule minds. Inferior to rule minds, and prevents you from taking an alternate capstone.


Is It Worth It?


        It most certainly is not! Towering ego and touch treatment are devastating losses, and you get very little to compensate them. I’ll never recommend thought eater.
