# Tiefling

Tiefling


        Much like aasimar, tieflings almost always appear on “best of” lists because of the staggering number of racial heritages available to them. For mesmerists, demon-spawn tieflings (+2 Strength/Charisma, -2 Intelligence) and div-spawn tieflings (+2 Dexterity/Charisma, -2 Intelligence) are unquestionably the way to go; they even gain some decent skill modifiers and spell-like abilities, to boot.


Pros
	Cons
	Great stat arrays. Again, stick with div- or demon-spawn tieflings; kyton- and rakshasa-spawn tieflings are also acceptable, although a hit to Wisdom is mathematically worse than a hit to Intelligence.
	None. Tieflings are less OP as mesmerists than they are as other classes because of their lack of in-house favored class bonus, but they’re still quite strong. Let the Satanic Panic commence.
	Awesome alternate racial traits. Maw or claw, scaled skin, prehensile tail—tieflings get some pretty cool alternate racial traits. You might want to grab pass for human, though, because that gives you access to…
	

	…the human favored class bonus. Most native outsiders have an option to be treated as humanoids for some purposes, and those purposes include favored class bonuses. The human favored class bonus is pretty good, albeit limited to 6 levels before you run out of runway.
	

	Racial darkvision.
