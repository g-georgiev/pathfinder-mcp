Paladin Spellcasting
So we don’t get many spells, we don’t get lots of caster level, so what do we have? Weird spells and early access. I won’t be looking at every spell, but I’ll point out the ones that are worth preparing each day. Remember, you are a prepared caster, and can swap your loadout for the problems before you.
