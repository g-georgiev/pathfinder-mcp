# Ability Scores

Ability Scores
So what STATS does our Shaman need to complete all of these various roles? Surely they are incredibly different in execution? No? They are literally all the same? Well alright then, I guess tell me exactly what I need for my ability scores.


* Strength (10): Well unless you plan on being in melee, which I suppose you can do just…don’t really have much reason to, you don’t honestly need strength for like…anything! You are a fullcaster! You can get by with a stick and some leaves because you can turn them into dinosaurs. 10 is fine and honestly, if you need to dump it, you absolutely should.
* Dexterity (10-14): So as a Shaman, you can wear armor…so you don’t really need that, and your familiar can give you a pretty strong bump to initiative…so you don’t need that either. You don’t even really need it for attacks! If you get this to 14, that's more than fine but honestly so long as its not negative (because of your bad reflex save) you are in the clear.
* Constitution (14-16): We have a bad fort save and not a super huge amount of health. Now we don’t have a TERRIBLE health mind you, but not a super huge amount, so its nice to get a little bit of a bonus. I wouldn’t suggest having this below a 14, but it doesn’t need to be much higher either!
* Intelligence (16 or 8-10)*: So there is ONE SOLE REASON to have a high intelligence and that is Arcane Enlightenment, the most infamous shaman hex from the Lore spirit. Your ability to cast the spells is based on your int, so if you are planning on picking up high level wizard spells, you NEED to have enough int for them to function…which also means that you will never have a reason to have your int above 19. That said, if you aren’t planning on using Arcane Enlightenment, you don’t really need INT at all but…its also a REALLY good hex you basically want every day. If you are abusing the lore spirit, start it at 15-16 and raise it every so often. If not, its your second dump stat.
* Wisdom (18+): THIS IS YOUR MAIN CASTING STAT. THIS IS ALSO YOUR MAIN DC STAT. THIS ALSO DETERMINES A BUNCH OF OTHER DUMB THINGS. This should always be your highest stat, if you can only have one good stat, its this stat. Wisdom should start at 18 and it should also be the first stat you boost every time. We are big wise.
* Charisma (16+): So surprisingly, you need this! There are a lot of hexes and spirit abilities that scale their uses per day based on your charisma! You cannot dump charisma and this should be the second stat you level up. Probably want it to start at a 16, or 14 at the ABSOLUTE lowest.
