# Class Overview

Class Overview
Okay so the shaman is…a weird class at worst and a wild ride at best. Supposedly a combination of the witch and the oracle, it feels far closer to a combination between the witch and the DRUID boasting a lot of the witches aesthetic along with a spell list comprised of a lot of nature-y shit.

Speaking of that spell list: it’s one of the worst in the game. Not unsavagable, but the shaman is NOTORIOUS For having a below par set of choices to work with, and has a lot fewer hexes than the witch to make up for it. They don’t even get access to major hexes or grand hexes! Which means that the shaman is bad right?

Absolutely fucking not. The shaman is one of, if not THE most versatile fullcasters in the game, and while they have fewer hexes at base, by the power of the spirits they can fucking SWAP hexes. That spell list? We can change it up every day and add spells from other lists with our FCB…so here is a guide to apparently, one of the best fullcasters in the game. Yeah. Wild right?
