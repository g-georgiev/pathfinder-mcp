# Multiclassing

Multiclassing
So…honestly? Okay with a lot of spellcasters I warn you that multiclassing is not necessarily the best option and you should only dip a level or two to get the most benefit but for the shaman…I  honestly don’t see the benefit? 

No i’m entirely serious. There isn’t much that interacts with wandering spirit, your familiar is your familiar, and like you have armor so you don’t NEED monk AC. I’m sure someone can come up with a reason or build that involves multiclassing but as far as I can tell its just not worth it unless you are doing a niche build so just…uh…don’t.
