# Races

Races
For the shaman specifically, stats are important but whats just as if not more important is the spell access! To be 5/5 or higher, a race needs to give you access to spells from a different class or something just as good! Moreover, I will be treating any race that grants access to “spells known” as if it lets you use them on your spell list, because otherwise its very silly and confusing.
(6/5) Races
* Drow Noble (+4 DEX, +2 INT/WIS/CHA, -2 CON): Somehow, it is the best race possible. Drow nobles take a hit to Con, which isn’t ideal but in exchange get a bonus to all mental stats, a bunch of SLA’s that we love (because remember, we don’t get many spells and very few of high level), and BALLER spell resistance. 
   * Abilities Scores: You get a penalty to CON which sucks but in exchange get a huge boost to Dex, and a boost to all of your mental stats.
   * Spell Resistance: 11+ Character Level is A LOT and there are going to be a fair amount of spells that just fizzle out on you if your DM isn’t careful.
   * So Many Spell Like Abilities: I’m going to list them below but just know that you get so many, your caster level is your character level, and they are all based off of your charisma, which should be decently high. This gets wilder if for some reason you DM allows you to change some of these spells. And remember, for every spell that you have here, that’s another spell you DON’T have to take.
      * Constant: Detect Magic (0th)
      * At-Will: Dancing Lights (0th), Deeper Darkness (3rd), faerie fire (1st), featherfall (1st), Levitate (2nd)
      * 1/day: Divine Favor (1st), Dispel Magic (3rd), Suggestion (2nd)


(5/5) Races
* Human (+2 ANY): You have the best favored class bonus, skilled to help make up for your ABYSMAL skill points, and a bonus feat to make you even more broken. Great option!
* Half-Orc (+2 ANY) [Sacred Tattoo / Skilled]: With sacred tattoo and the fate’s favored trait, you can enjoy +2 to all saves, and again, skilled is amazing. Add on human FCB? We are off to the races.
* Half-Elf (+2 ANY) [Kindred-Raised or Spell Resistance]: Whatever stat you want, cleric spell FCB and possible extra bonus to charisma or spell resistance, or even just skill focus! We love it here! Also, don’t forget to pick up paragon surge somehow to have some fun.
* Aasimar [Scion Of Humanity]: Human FCB, great stats, free skills, and a cool SLA! Also no penalties!!!! What? Yeah! This is super duper mega cool! We love our angels.
   * Base Aasimar (+2 WIS/CHA)
   * Archon-Blooded (+2 CON/WIS)
   * Garuda-Blooded (+2 DEX/WIS)
* Variant Tiefling [Pass For Human/Prehensile TAil]: Cool SLA’s, Darkvision, Human fcb for free cleric spells, and a tail that can hold rods and wands and stuff! We love it here! Note, that all of these give a penalty to some mental stat, so ONLY PLAY IT if you arent using arcane enlightenment.
   * Asura-Spawn (+2 DEX/WIS, -2 INT)
   * Demodand-Spawn (+2 CON/WIS, -2 INT)
* Samsaran (+2 WIS/INT, -2 CON) [Mystic Past Life]: Mystic Past Life! The ability to snag spells, at level, from any divine class, most of which are different from you, is so powerful, it bumps this race up. There is an entire guide on this. It’s that good.
* Shabti (+2 CON/CHA) [Facsimile]: So you’ll notice this doesn’t get a bonus to wisdom…so why does it get 5/5? Well because by taking advantage of facsimile, you get access to both the Psychic and Cleric lists via your FCB, meaning that you get UNPARALLELED versatility when it comes to picking out new and interesting spells…which remember, is the main thing we care about. It also gives us bonuses to two stats that we like so…yeah duh.
* Syrinx (+2 WIS, -2 DEX): So this one does NOT get any additional spells? So why in the world is it 5/5. Well you get a 60ft fly speed with average maneuverability at level 1 and a bonus to wisdom with no penalties to your other mental stats. For a caster, this keeps them safe from 80% of threats, and is basically as good as most spells. We have other tricks.
* Grippli (+2 DEX/WIS, -2 STR): Perfect stat block, can steal druid spells which is still great, and they get a climb speed. Our little frog shamans have it made honestly.
* Base Undine  (+2 DEX/WIS, -2 STR) [Mostly Human / Nereid Fascination]: Great statblock, Human FCB, And a fun fascination ability.
* Vine Leshy (+2 CON/WIS, -2 INT): Choose wood or nature as your main spirit and pick up druid spells. AGAIN this is great SO LONG AS YOU ARE NOT USING ARCANE ENLIGHTENMENT.
* Ironsoul Oread (+2 CON/WIS, -2 DEX) [Mostly Human / Oread Gem Magic]: Human FCB, Wisdom and Constitution bonus, you don’t lose anything from our go to stats AND gem magic is a cool little buff to your normal spells. Luckily most stone spells are different enough and generally helpful enough that this isn’t a total waste.
* Munavri (+4 Dex, +2 CON/INT/WIS/CHA, -2 STR): Munavri is a dumb race that gets a bonus to all stats, except the one we don’t care about, get’s us free spell resistance that isn’t bad, and once per day can use spell trigger items for classes she shouldn’t be able to so...yeah no great stuff. 
(4/5) Races
* Bonus to Wisdom, No penalty to mental stats or CON.
* Off List Spells FCB, No penalty to mental stats.
(3/5) Races
* Bonus to mental stat, no penalties to mental stat.
(2/5) Races
* No penalties to Wisdom or Charisma.
(1/5) Races
* Penalty to Wisdom or Charisma
