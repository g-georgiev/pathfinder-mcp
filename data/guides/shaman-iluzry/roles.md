# Roles

Roles
Try to have a 16 in your main stat and a 14 in your secondary stat. All of the following reviews are for the Base Class, without any archetypes, multiclasses, or Prestige Classes which would augment their ability. 

As far as roles go, the Shaman can do just about ANYTHING…okay. Like need summoning? It has Summon Nature’s Ally. Need physical attack? Well it has a spear and ¾ Bab if it needs to. It CAN do anything okay-ish but it has a few places were it excels, and all of them require the same stats. So instead of listing every role it could potentially be good enough at, we are just going to talk about where the shaman SHINES with little effort, and the stats that support it.
Debuffer
As a shaman you get access to hexes, which are pretty powerful fuck you tools, including everyones favorites, Evil Eye/Misfortune/Chant (Cackle), on top of your spell list being full of curses and save or sucks. If you want to make someones’ life hell, the shaman is incredibly talented at being a pain in the ass.
Battlefield Control
Lots of control spells you steal from the druid list, like entangle, and creation spells to make the power of nature your own. Really this is one of the reasons why the class feels more like a druid/witch hybrid than an oracle/witch, and that's a compliment!
Buff/Support
Shaman hexes are full of nifty buffs just as much as debuffs, you get access to a LOT of buffing abilities and various support effects, and great spells to make channeling the power of the spirits to your homies fantastic.
Healing
So while we don’t really get any specific healing abilities (like channel energy) but we can always Wandering Spirit into the Life or Restoration spirits to have what we need! On top of that, we have a surprising amount of recovery spells, which makes us a pretty damn good healer.
