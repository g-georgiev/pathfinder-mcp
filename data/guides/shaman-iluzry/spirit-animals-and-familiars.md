# Spirit Animals and Familiars

Spirit Animals and Familiars
Hey so your Spirit Animal is a familiar! Key differences are that your familiar will be treated as a native outsider when talking about the spells that affect it (this does not impact any traits a familiar may have due to its race, just what spells are limited by racial type). It can also deliver hexes that use touch!!!!


So you probably are wondering, okay well what should I choose for MY spirit animal huh? Well good for you, I am a familiar addict…so I made a guide for it. 

Special Notes: Remember that your familiar is your spell book! YOU DO NOT WANT YOUR SPELLBOOK IN HARM'S WAY. IF IT DIES IT IS EXPENSIVE! So we cannot use it as like…our battle beast. That isn’t to say it can’t help us, just remember, we don’t want it to be DIRECTLY in the enemies face.
