# The Chassis

The Chassis
* (3/5) Hit Die - D8: So as you’ll come to find, all of a shaman’s base abilities are HILAROUSLY mid. This health? Enough to not get you killed but not so much to be praised for.
* BAB - ¾ (Medium): For full casters, this is still REALLY good. Yes I know the Druid and Cleric get this, and yes its still good because of it. Now why is this blue for us? Because in most cases we will NOT be in melee. We are not BUILT to be in melee but when we have to use something with BAB, this is still nice to have.
* (3/5) Skills - 4+Int: So this is…er not the best. Same as the druid, you also aren’t an int based character..and have little reason to invest into your skills...so...not like actively terrible but also not super great.
   * Class Skills Worth Paying attention to:
      * Know. Nature/Planes/Religion (Int): Knowing things is useful.
      * Spellcraft (Int): Understanding spells is useful
   * Non Class Skills Worth Paying Attention To:
      * Know. Arcana/Dungeoneering (Int): Knowing things continues to be useful.
      * Perception (Wis): Most rolled skill in the game, you will use it, no matter the class, and not having it will get you in trouble at least once. AND YOU ARE WISDOM BASED!!!! Take it, by any means that you can.
* (3/5) Saves - Good Will: So good news is that will is an important stat and you are a wisdom based character, so you should basically never fail a will save. Bad news? Fort is also important…rip.
* (3/5) Weapon And Armor Proficiencies: Simple weapons (bleh) and medium armor (ooooo). We can’t really attack with anything BUT getting medium armor proficiency is GREAT, so we can wear that mithral fullplate/celestial plate armor, and since we are a divine caster, we have no issues!!!! YAy defense!
