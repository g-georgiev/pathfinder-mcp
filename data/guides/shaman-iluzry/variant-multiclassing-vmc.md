# Variant Multiclassing (VMC)

Variant Multiclassing (VMC)
VMC on the other hand is possibly viable. Now we actually have a LOT of feats we like. Split hex, ritual hex, dreamed secrets, spirit talker, yadda yadda, so while we CAN do this, we definitely can’t pussyfoot around with it. So whatever we get? Has got to be good.


* (4/5) Cavalier
   * Tactician is a really strong power, challenge is whatever damage wise, but if you plan on being a fighty type it is absolutely wonderful and the order bonuses are INSANE. Order of the Blossom is great for save penalties and fun SLA’s with fey lore, while Order of the Dragon is great for being a team player, and Order of the Reins makes you an animal handling god.
* (5/5) Sorcerer
   * Bloodline powers can be insanely lit and the main reason I’d choose this over eldritch heritage is just because well we may not have the HIGHEST charisma and for people who aren’t building around it, this is a legitimately good option. The Arcane and Astral bloodlines come to mind…
* (4/5) Wizard
   * Fully leveled school powers!!! ARCANE DISCOVERIES!!! Sure the cantrip isn't useful but its very rare that we get an ability that is ACTUALLY good around level 15, and guess what? These are actually good! PArticularly good for Speaker For The Past shamans that got rid of their familiars for dope abilities.
* (4/5) Oracle
   * Technically does not have you taking a level in another class that gives you mysteries, so you don’t have to make them match. Revelations are really good, not much more to say on that, even if we only get a few and they are very low level.
