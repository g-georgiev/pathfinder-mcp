# Roles and Ability Scores

Roles and Ability Scores
….So usually I just have my copy paste template here but I’m going to level with you. You are a sorcerer. You use charisma. You need charisma. You fucking live, breathe and ooze charisma. You have two class features, and both of them scale almost entirely on your charisma. This isn’t really a question of what your primary stat is (which is charisma) but more what you are doing with your physical stats. So I’m going to do this section a bit different than usual and just...give you each score.


* Strength (STR): Are you going into melee? If so, first of all, WHY? I assume you are just dipping sorcerer, but for anyone who is here seriously...uh well you probably shouldn’t but if you are going to this is likely important. Otherwise? Trash. We smite gods, not lift weights. Doesn’t matter if you aren’t in melee.
* Dexterity (DEX): Always good to have. This is our AC, Initiative, and sometimes even our attacks depending on what spells we are using. This should likely be one of the first physical stats you bump up, because it’s just so gosh darn helpful. Probably okay at 16-18
* Constitution (CON): HEY remember how we have GARBAGE health and GARBAGE fort saves? This fixes both of those. So yes, dex is super important, but this...keeps you alive? So you choose what is most important to you. Don’t want this below a 14, the higher the better.
* Intelligence (INT): So intelligence gets you more skills, which you desperately need, and helps with knowledge or spellcraft, which are both useful. Can’t say this is ever like required to put points into but, if you can it’s nice. You’ll need at least a 13 to pick up Versatile Spontaneity, which you definitely super want, but not much else.  Not much need for above a 13-14.
* Wisdom (WIS): So like this boosts your will save...which is your only good save...and perception...which is a skill so most of its scaling just comes from your level...so uh...I mean besides that not much reason for it I’m afraid. Just don’t let it go below 8-10. Try not to have a negative modifier.
* Charisma (CHA): THIS IS YOUR GOD. YOU ARE THE SMILE THAT LIGHTS THE SKY AND YOUR WILL CREATES THE WORLD. Seriously though, this is entirely something you need to constantly keep up. You always boost this stat first. This WILL be your highest stat. Don’t go below 19. You want this in the ceiling. More charisma is always good.




For those of you who wanted to know what role the sorcerer could fill or for those of you who think the sorcerer is only good at blasting, let me be clear. YOU SHOULD BE A JACK OF ALL TRADES. You aim is to build up a spell repertoire of abilities that can handle just about any problem you run into, and look cool as hell doing it. So what can our spells do? Buff / Debuff / Battlefield Control / Blast / Utility. And you are going to do ALL of them. If anyone tells you that the sorcerer is only good for blasting, take over their body and curse their entire family line.
