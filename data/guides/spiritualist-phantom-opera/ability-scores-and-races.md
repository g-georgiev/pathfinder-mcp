# Ability Scores and Races

Ability Scores and Races


        The spiritualist has a wide variety of builds available to it, but before you even get thinking about those, you need to answer one simple question: Wisdom or Charisma? The base spiritualist is Wisdom-based, but the Fractured Mind archetype swaps their casting modifier and a few fluffy spell-like abilities - essentially putting you in a position where Wisdom or Charisma is purely a personal decision and every race that grants a bonus to Wisdom or Charisma is an option for a spiritualist.


Wisdom
* Applies to Will saves.
* Applies to important skill checks like Perception and Sense Motive.
* Required if you want to take the Fated archetype or any future archetype that clashes with Fractured Mind.
* Can leave Charisma as a dump stat; better for MAD (multi-attribute dependent) builds and low point buy campaigns or poor rolls.


Charisma
* Better for multiclassing or “X to Y” builds.
* Applies to social skills like Diplomacy and Intimidate, as well as Use Magic Device; can easily fill the role of party “face.”
* Broader range of available races.
* Fractured Mind spell-like abilities are often more useful than the base spiritualist ones.
