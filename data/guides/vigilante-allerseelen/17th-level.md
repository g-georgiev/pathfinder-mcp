# 17th Level

17th Level


Any Guise (Su): A fitting capstone social talent for the vigilante class, any guise allows you to impersonate anyone at any time. It’s hard to overstate just how much of an edge this gives you in deception, manipulation, and infiltration, especially given that the ability is supernatural and therefore not subject to dispel magic, detect magic, or most other divinations that would reveal who you truly are. If you have many guises and everyman, this talent needs to be your 17th-level pick, no questions asked. I said no questions.
