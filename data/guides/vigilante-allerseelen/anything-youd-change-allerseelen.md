# Anything You’d Change, Allerseelen?

Anything You’d Change, Allerseelen?


Really not! Apart from the inherent thematic silliness of playing both Clark Kent and Superman in the same character and watching no one ever put two and two together, the vigilante is an exceptionally mechanically solid class. The power and flexibility of social and vigilante talents feels highly similar to the design direction that Paizo eventually went in with 2e, and it feels fantastic to be able to put together whatever kind of fighter or rogue you want, complete with great social prowess, good saves, and a bunch of skill ranks. I do wish that vigilantes got more support for ranged combat styles, but that’s something you could potentially homebrew with your GM if you really wanted to.
