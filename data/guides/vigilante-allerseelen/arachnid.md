# Arachnid

Arachnid


        (2) Heightened Senses (Ex): Uncanny dodge and improved uncanny dodge got a green rating in the vigilante talent section, so that feels fair here. Could this be a not-so-subtle homage to a certain friendly neighborhood Spidey Sense?


        (6) Shoot Webs (Ex): Away, web! Tanglefoot bags are surprisingly effective alchemical items, but what’s no surprise is that they get better with scaling save DCs. I’m frankly not sure why any part of this ability is tied to Constitution, as it doesn’t seem to match the vigilante’s general design ethos, but we’ll roll with the punches here. Remember that Huge and larger creatures aren’t affected by tanglefoot bags at all!


        (12) Web Specialist (Ex): A climb speed is fun, but nothing you couldn’t have gotten elsewhere. As for your web swinging action, well, I hate (I hate) that they cost a use of shoot webs. For my money, you could package the 12th- and 18th-level arachnid abilities in with the 6th-level ability and I wouldn’t even blink. I mean, fly is a 3rd-level spell. Do we really have to pump the brakes on an ability that allows you to “fly” at your base speed at 18th level? That just seems needlessly cruel. Yeah. We’re gonna third-party this jawn. Hang on.


        (18) Web Master (Ex): So you wanted to be Spiderman, did you? Well, Paizo went and spit on your childhood dreams, because you’ll only get this ability at the very end of your Pathfinder career. Let’s check out my rewritten arachnid specialization for something a little more spicy, shall we?
