# Dipping To Vigilante

Dipping To Vigilante


        Dips to vigilante don’t really work, unfortunately. Vigilante and social talents are obviously the most valuable resources the class has to offer, but since there’s no “Extra Vigilante Talent” or “Extra Social Talent” feat, you can’t jump in, grab the class feature, then jump out again and continue purchasing vigilante talents with feats. Moreover, many of the best vigilante and social talents are gated behind vigilante levels, which makes dipping a non-starter. If you do dip, you have to be prepared to play the Iron Man angle, not concealing that your social and vigilante identities are one and the same—you simply won’t have the seamless guise bonuses to keep that secret. The level-gating in the class is real, though, and poses the larger problem.
