# Dwarf

Dwarf


        With the exception of the hardy racial trait, dwarves have nothing to recommend them as vigilantes. Their Charisma is generally too low to make good use of Intimidate, Bluff, Diplomacy, and Disguise, all of which are bread and butter skills for the class; those low Charisma scores also hurt their vigilante talent save DCs. I can’t in good conscience push you in this direction.


Pros
	Cons
	Hardy. A perennial favorite of dwarf PCs, the hardy racial trait gives you a +2 racial bonus to saves vs. poisons, spells, and spell-like abilities, which covers a huge array of effects.
	Exceptionally poor attribute bonuses. Remember what our criteria were? Bonuses to either Charisma, Dexterity, or Strength, and hopefully no penalties to Charisma or your chosen physical stat. Not only do dwarves not grant any relevant bonuses to physical or mental attributes, they also take a -2 Charisma penalty. That’s not as bad as it woulds be for a Charisma-based spellcaster, but it’s still not good.
	Darkvision.
	Pretty bad favored class bonus. Mundane crafting is unfortunately an exceptionally poor use of your time as a PC. Go get a crafting cohort or familiar, ya hippie.
