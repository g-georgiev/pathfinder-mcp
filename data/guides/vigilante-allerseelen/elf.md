# Elf

Elf


        Anything elves can do, half-elves can do better, I’m afraid. Half-elves can get the same weapon proficiencies and visual senses if they want, and have both a more flexible stat bonus and a more consistently useful favored class bonus. Do it for story reasons if you must, but it’s not mechanically optimal.


Pros
	Cons
	Some cool weapon proficiencies. Bonus weapon proficiencies fall a bit flat when the class is already proficient with all martial weapons, but elven curve blades and branched spears are undeniably great.
	Very, very narrow favored class bonus. Skill bonuses to move about stealthily in forested terrain are all well and good, but you run into the classic Ranger Syndrome of being in your favored terrain a small percentage of the time. Potentially green for Ironfang Invasion, but not much besides.
	Low-light vision, but no darkvision.
	No bonuses to Charisma, and a penalty to Constitution. Vigilantes are weak on their Fortitude saves, have a d8 HD (even avengers!), and generally need to feel comfortable in melee range. A hit to Constitution with bonuses to the vigilante’s usual dump stat (Intelligence) hurts.
