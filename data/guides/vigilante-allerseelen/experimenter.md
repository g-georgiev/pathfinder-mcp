# Experimenter

Experimenter


The Basics


        Part-alchemist vigilantes who gain the ability to brew potions and mutagens, craft constructs, and more.


        Versatility: +2
        Power: -1


        Stacks With: 


The Abilities


        (1) Class Skills: Alters class skills. Nothing but upside! Vigilantes only get two monster identification skills naturally, and with the ancestral enlightenment social talent, there’s a good incentive for investing at least one rank in each Knowledge skill. That only gets better with the lore master class feature coming up in a few levels.


        (1) Weapon and Armor Proficiency: Alters weapon and armor proficiencies. With no martial weapons, no medium armor, and no vigilante specialization, experimenters are naturally going to fall into high-Dexterity Weapon Finesse builds that don’t deal nearly as much damage as either stalkers or avengers.


        (1) Forbidden Science (Ex): Replaces vigilante specialization. Brute, darklantern, experimenter…man, vigilante archetypes sure have a thing for screwing you over with involuntary transformations, don’t they? The skill check bonuses are good, and I’m a big fan of mutagens, but it’s really hard to countenance an ability that forces you to become confused for a bunch of rounds, causes you to ignore “act normally” results, and randomly determines the attribute penalties your mutagen imposes. If you can get past that, mutagens are obviously amazing. Mutagens, cognatogens, fey mutagens, and rasugens are all worth their salt, depending on the build you’re trying to create. Just…pump your Will save as high as you can if you’re playing an experimenter, and guard specifically against fear effects, which are the most common save-based abilities that will trigger an unwanted transformation. Iron Will, Improved Iron Will, Eagle’s Resolve, and Unimpeachable Honor are decent places to start from a feat perspective.


        (1) Mutagenic Change (Ex): Replaces 1st-level social identity. Cool! You get quick change (a talent that’s compulsory for builds that conceal their social/vigilante identities) a few levels early, provided you have a mutagen on hand. As a reminder, you can brew those with 1 hour of effort.


        (3) Brew Potion: Replaces unshakable. Ohhh, what I wouldn’t give for alchemical allocation right now! With this class feature, you can grab potions at their very lowest caster level, then mass-produce them for your team at your caster level. Given your Craft (Alchemy) bonuses and the full investment you’ll be making in the skill (right? right?) it should be a cinch to create your own cottage industry here. And as a replacement for unshakable? Please and thank you.


        (5) Lore Master (Ex): Replaces startling appearance. I’d rather get bardic knowledge than lore master, but it’s undeniably lovely to be able to take 10 on all Knowledge checks—and take 20 a few times a day on truly critical checks.


        (11) Craft Construct: Replaces frightening appearance. Constructs may be monstrously expensive, but there’s no denying that having one on your team can amount to an entirely separate cohort. Just, uh…make friends with someone who can cast greater make whole.


        (17) Mutable Mutagen: Replaces stunning appearance. It’s a crying shame that you need to wait this long to turn your mutagen into an all-day affair—alchemists get their hours/level mutagens at 14th, after all—but I suppose that’s life. Anyway, this is a great upgrade. You don’t need me to tell you that. 


Is It Worth It?


        Absent a vigilante specialization and access to martial weapons/medium armor, experimenters may not hit as hard as normal vigilantes, even stalkers, but what they lack in raw stopping power, they more than make up in versatility, skill use, and team crafting. Mutagens can be turned into dang near anything you want, whether that’s a hulking ball of claws and teeth, a brainiac on par with Einstein, or anything in between. I’ll go ahead with the obligatory plug for levels as an evangelist of Haagenti. I mean, sure, he’s a demon lord, but good grief, an additional +2 to every attribute while your mutagen is active and unlimited oils of life are…good. Too good to ignore.
