Feline (Allerseelen’s Rewrite)


        I think there’s probably a more elegant way to balance the feline wildsoul than three middling abilities sandwiched around “POUNCE CAT GO BRRRRRR,” but I don’t much feel like writing it. Feline pounce is strong enough that the entire natural course rockets up the charts. Let it go, man.


Is It Worth It?


        The natural courses stratify themselves nicely into Feline > Falconine > Arachnid > Ursine. My rewrites bring them all into alignment with one another, somewhere in the high green, low blue zone. It’s a fun archetype, and I’d hate for it to be relegated to the trash heap, but as it stands, only feline and falconine are really worth it.
