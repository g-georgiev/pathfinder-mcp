# Half-Elf Double Scion

Half-Elf Double Scion


The Basics


        Half-elf vigilantes who gain some optional vigilante talents, but lose essentially nothing else.


        Versatility: -1
        Power: -1


        Stacks With: 


The Abilities


        (1) Dual Heritage: Alters dual identity. Sure. Human by day, elf by night. Fine.


        (2) Half-Elf Double Scion Talents: Alters vigilante talents. Nothing but options, babyyy! (Mediocre options, but still, options!)


      * (10) Arrogant Strike (Ex): It’s fun to be able to call your crits like this, but realistically, even crit-fishing builds have no idea whether they’ll threaten enough to make the ability worthwhile. Plus, you can only use it only 1/day, when you pick it up at 10th level, which seems excessively stingy. I’d say just take Critical Focus if you really want to get your confirmation rolls on point.
      * (2) Slick Step (Ex): Close the gap will be the superior vigilante talent in almost all circumstances, but slick step still isn’t terrible.
      * (2) Elven Weapon Command (Ex): In a world where the ancestral arms and weapon familiarity alternate racial traits exist, elven weapon command is essentially a trap that burns a vigilante talent on Weapon Focus, which is *checks notes* bad, bad, bad.


Is It Worth It?


        Not truly, no. Even though the archetype gives up essentially nothing, the fact that it alters dual identity and vigilante talents prevents you from taking other, better archetypes that also alter those class features. Save your squeeze for better juice.
