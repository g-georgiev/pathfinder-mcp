# Half-Orc

Half-Orc


        The usual half-orc/half-elf/human holy trinity stands strong! If you’re looking for a race with darkvision, bonuses to Intimidate, strong saves, and great weapon proficiencies, the half-orc is a solid pick. It doesn’t have quite as much to recommend it without a useable favored class bonus, but the human favored class bonus is the second-strongest after the halfling favored class bonus. We can’t all be halflings.


Pros
	Cons
	Floating stat bonus. Floating stat bonuses are the name of the game for half-elves, half-orcs, and humans, and they’re flexible enough to let you build any kind of vigilante you want.
	None. Half-orcs are typically a very strong choice.
	Sacred Tattoo + Fate’s Favored: A classic powergamer move that grants a +2 luck bonus to all saves. Half-orc, babyyyy!
	

	Great weapon proficiencies. Vigilantes already do well for themselves with weapons, but half-orcs can get cool weapons like the orc hornbow much more easily than other races.
	

	One decent favored class bonus. The native half-orc favored class bonus is hot trash (unshakable is your worst class feature by a country mile) but as a half-orc, you have access to the human favored class bonus. Use that, I beg of you.
	

	Racial darkvision. You can always upgrade to 120 feet with the Dragon Sight trait!
