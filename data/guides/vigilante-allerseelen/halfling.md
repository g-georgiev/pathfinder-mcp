# Halfling

Halfling


        Halflings are the superlative race for vigilantes. Especially for stalker vigilantes, there must be an extremely compelling reason why you’re not playing a halfling! They’ve got all the right stat bonuses for Dexterity-based vigilantes, great racial traits, Small size, and an unbelievable favored class bonus that stands head and shoulders above every other race in the game. Y’all come get some.


Pros
	Cons
	Insanely good favored class bonus. Every once in a while, you run into an favored class bonus where you ask, “What was Paizo thinking when they created this?” The gathlain kineticist favored class bonus is a great example of mechanics that prompt this question; this is another. There is no force in Pathfinder that can give you more social talents, save this favored class bonus. Three bonus social talents over the course of your career means you can pick up the entire any guise line for free, snag hidden magic + ancestral enlightenment + companion to the lonely for great bonuses, take your renown game to the next level, or more. It is the preeminent favored class bonus in the game.
	No racial low-light vision or darkvision.
	Great stat bonuses. Bonuses to Dexterity and Charisma are a perfect fit for most Weapon Finesse and stalker vigilante builds. Note that because the lethal grace vigilante talent explicitly forces you to use your Strength modifier for damage and the halfling gives you a penalty to Strength, you may want to stick with the classic Weapon Finesse + an agile weapon instead of using lethal grace.
	

	Good racial traits. Fleet of Foot and Adaptable Luck are the only two alternatives that I would consistently recommend. Fate’s Favored + Adaptable Luck is a classic combo that can give you a +3 luck bonus on most d20 rolls 3/day.
	

	Small size. Small-sized races do great as rogues and stalker vigilantes, where their smaller weapon damage dice aren’t nearly as relevant when stacked against all the precision d6s or d8s they’re slinging around. Everything else—AC, Stealth, attack bonuses—is gravy.
