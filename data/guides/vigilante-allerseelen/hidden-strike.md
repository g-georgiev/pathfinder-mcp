# Hidden Strike

Hidden Strike


(10) Mighty Ambush (Ex): Stalker vigilantes are truly the masters of taking foes out before they get the chance to react! Pending a failed Fortitude save, mighty ambush simply drops enemies unconscious, whereupon you can coup de grace at your leisure if they’re isolated from their friends. Silent dispatch is a useful adjunct to mighty ambush, since it helps fewer enemies become aware that an attack is taking place, and up close and personal is practically mandatory so that you can attempt to knock each enemy out (even those that are aware of you!) at least once in a combat. Doing so greatly shifts the odds of any encounter in your favor.


(2) Foe Collision (Ex): Unlike many vigilante talents that modify hidden strike, foe collision will work even if your enemy is aware that you’re there, but you’ll still need a means of denying them their Dexterity bonus to AC, such as Shatter Defenses, dirty tricks to blind someone, or a feint—mere flanking won’t cut it. At any rate, a solid hidden strike talent for dealing damage in a wider area.
(4) Throat Jab (Ex): The 1/day per enemy restriction is a little onerous, but even so, throat jab is a fantastic way of taking out isolated sentries or spellcasters without anyone being the wiser. Pair with the silent dispatch vigilante talent for maximum effect.


(2) Leave an Opening (Ex): A little extra damage after a successful hidden strike doesn’t excite me too much, although it pairs nicely with vital punishment and animal patron (snake), if that’s your jam.
