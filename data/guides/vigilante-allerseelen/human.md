# Human

Human


        I wonder whether humans ever get tired of being Paizo’s clear favorite among all the races? The favored class bonus differential between halflings and humans is the only thing holding our native species back from the purple rating among the core races. More feats and skill ranks never, ever hurt.


Pros
	Cons
	Floating stat bonus. Floating stat bonuses are the name of the game for half-elves, half-orcs, and humans, and they’re flexible enough to let you build any kind of mesmerist you want.
	No racial darkvision or low-light vision.
	Bonus feat. Even though many vigilante talents approximate feats, it can still take a bit of massaging to get a combat-ready build up and running. That being the case, the human bonus feat can buy you a whole two levels of advancement ahead of other races.
	

	Extra skill ranks. Why, don’t mind if I do! 6 + Intelligence is already quite good, but many vigilantes will need to dump Intelligence to accommodate other stats. Nice to be able to buy back that lost rank.
	

	Good favored class bonus. Seamless guise bonuses aren’t top priority for most vigilantes, but with seamless shapechanger, you can definitely become very hard to spot while disguised.
