# Is It Worth It?

Is It Worth It?


        Man, what a tricky question. Look: y’all know that I’m an inquisitor guy, through and through. It’s my bread and butter class. But I look at this and I think: “Yes, you’ve got social talents, but absent so many vigilante talents, how is this distinct or better than the inquisitor?” You’d have to spend four talents (stalwart, harsh judgment, stern gaze, discern lies) just buying back inquisitor powers, and you’d still have poorer skill ranks and domain choices, no monster lore, no cunning initiative, no teamwork feats, no bane, no second or third judgments…it just seems like you’re far better off playing an inquisitor if you want to be an inquisitor. All you’d be missing out on is zealot smite and the infiltration abilities you could get from many guises.
