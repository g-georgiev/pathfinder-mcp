# Many Guises Line

Many Guises Line


5th Level


Many Guises (Ex): This talent opens up sooo many doors for the vigilante. Who pays attention to the gardener or the scullery maid, after all? Paired with hidden magic and some glamered equipment, you can stroll into almost any location on Golarion, fully ready for combat, with no one knowing or caring who you are. It’s really quite extraordinary.




11th Level


Everyman (Ex): Everyman feels like a bit of a step back from many guises, mostly because there may be only limited scenarios in which you need to impersonate a specific farmer, laborer, or peasant, rather than just creating a new, generic mundane identity. The bonuses to Bluff checks can be quite useful, however, because again, who pays much attention to the stonemason, farmer, beggar, or cook as they walk around the grounds of that heavily fortified castle?
