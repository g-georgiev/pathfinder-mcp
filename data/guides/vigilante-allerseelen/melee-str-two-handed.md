# Melee (STR, Two-Handed)

Melee (STR, Two-Handed)


        If you’re thinking of a classic Strength build for your PC, the avenger vigilante specialization is the logical move. Avengers get full BAB, and they’ve got a number of exclusive talents that predispose them to lower Dexterity scores, higher Strength scores, and more iterative attacks. Shield of blades is the ideal 2nd-level vigilante talent to take as a two-handed Strength build, because it gives you both offense (free Power Attack, and the chance to retrain that feat if you already took it at 1st level) and defense (shield bonuses to AC that scale with your Power Attack attack penalty). All you truly need to get started down this path is Power Attack and heavy armor proficiency, making it an ideal path for newcomers to Pathfinder.
