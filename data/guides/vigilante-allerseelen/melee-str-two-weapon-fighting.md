# Melee (STR, Two-Weapon Fighting)

Melee (STR, Two-Weapon Fighting)


        Woah, woah, woah! Don’t let that red rating fool you: vigilantes, especially avengers, are as effective as other classes at Two-Weapon Fighting playstyles if you can get the right feats to them. The problem is that vigilantes don’t have extra feats to spare—barring those granted by vigilante talents—and their vigilante talent lineup bizarrely only supports sword and board builds. Shield of fury only grants Two-Weapon Fighting and the ability to ignore Dexterity prerequisites if one of your weapons is a shield, and there aren’t any other TWF vigilante talents. My advice would therefore be to make peace with the sword and board TWF build (it’s not so bad—check out the Bladecatcher build below the fold!)
