# Melee (Sword and Board)

Melee (Sword and Board)


Shield Focus: It’s no one’s favorite feat, but you can’t really get anywhere in shield tactics without Shield Focus. That’s the way that it goes.
Shield Slam / Shield Master / Bashing Finish: Lots and lots of good stuff in these three feats, although obviously only sword-and-board avengers will be interested.
Two-Weapon Fighting / Improved / Greater: Although I still think it’s a little silly that sword and board builds have to get the TWF feats in addition to all the shield feats, Paizo wrote the system that way for what I can only assume are good reasons. You’ll need these if you’re going to deal maximum damage as a sword and board avenger.


Stumbling Bash / Toppling Bash: Some free debuffing and combat maneuvers, although frankly you probably won’t have the build space to take them, given the many feats that sword and board builds need to succeed.


Improved Shield Bash: Granted for free by the shield of fury vigilante talent.
