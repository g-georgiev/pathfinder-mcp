# Melee (Two-Handed)

Melee (Two-Handed)


Furious Focus: An easy picks for two-handed vigilantes of any specialization. You want the extra damage that Power Attack will provide, and you want your attack rolls to be as juicy as possible, even if your first attack is the most likely to hit anyway. Note that you have to have at least one iterative attack that takes the Power Attack penalty in order to use the shield of blades vigilante talent.


Shield of Swings: Shield of Swings is a decent feat for two-handed builds that want some defensive options. Your damage output will definitely suffer, but against low-level trash mobs that are going to die easily and have a hard time hitting your improved AC, the tradeoff might be worth it.
