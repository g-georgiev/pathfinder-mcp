# Melee (Two-Weapon Fighting)

Melee (Two-Weapon Fighting)


Possessed Hand / Hand’s Autonomy: Who knew that two-weapon fighters could get so much mileage out of detaching a hand? In addition to a +1 insight bonus to attack and damage rolls and a reduction in TWF penalties, possessed hands can do some wacky, awesome things while you’re incapacitated.
Two-Weapon Fighting / Improved / Greater: Required for the playstyle, for obvious reasons.
Two-Weapon Rend: Once you have enough attacks, Two-Weapon Rend is essentially a free attack every round, which is fantastic.


Double Slice: You want full damage on off-hand attacks, thanks very much.
Heaven’s Step: You’ll need Step Up, Following Step, and Step Up and Strike to get the most mileage out of Heaven’s Step, but man, it’s a lethal combo for magehunters.
