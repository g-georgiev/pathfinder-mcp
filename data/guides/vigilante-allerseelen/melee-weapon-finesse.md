# Melee (Weapon Finesse)

Melee (Weapon Finesse)


Agile Maneuvers: Dexterity-based avengers might be interested in Agile Maneuvers, but they’re about the only ones. Stalkers probably shouldn’t bother because of their ¾ BAB progression.
Piranha Strike: As with Deadly Aim and Power Attack, avengers will want Piranha Strike; stalkers will most likely not.


Weapon Finesse + [Weapon] Grace / Bladed Brush / Dance of Chains / Dervish Dance: The lethal grace vigilante talent is the optimal means of building a finesse vigilante, whether avenger or stalker. Set your Dexterity as high as you can, park your Strength at 12-14 (or more, if you can afford it!) and let the talent do the rest—no need to go grabbing three feats to accomplish what one talent can accomplish.
