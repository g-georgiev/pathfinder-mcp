# Non-Magical Healing

Non-Magical Healing


Healer’s Hands / Incredible Healer / Signature Skill (Heal): Healer’s Hands is absolutely vital for non-magical healing builds, but whether you go for Incredible Healer and early-game healing or Signature Skill (Heal) for late-game healing is more or less up to you. Don’t forget to max out Knowledge (Planes) if you pick these up, though!


Acupuncture Specialist: Non-magical remove curse. That could be handy.
Improvisational Healer: Almost nicer for the potion benefits than for the Heal benefits! Nice not to have to tote around healer’s kits for treat deadly wounds, too.
Noble Scion (Scion of Peace): Scion of Peace is noteworthy for vigilantes because of the skill familiarity social talent, which lets you select four skills that you can always take 10 on. The power move here is obviously to select all Wisdom-based skills (Perception, Sense Motive, Heal, and whatever free pick you feel is relevant) and then benefit from being able to “take 13” on those skills in addition to the bonuses you get when you take 13 out of combat.
Pathologist: Non-magical neutralize poison and remove disease. Again, could be quite handy.


Acupuncture Specialist: You may not have the Wisdom necessary to really capitalize on Heal checks, but skill familiarity and social grace can help. It’s nice to be able to cast remove curse without any spell slots.
