# Quick Change Line

Quick Change Line


7th Level


Quick Change (Ex): If you’re trying to separate your vigilante and social identities, you need quick change, period. At base, changing between identities takes a full minute, which means that you simply will not be able to participate in combat if it breaks out while you’re in your social identity. Unacceptable, right? Well, as crappy as it would be to waste an entire round of combat changing identities, quick change decreases the amount of time you need to change by a factor of 10, and that simply can’t be ignored. If you’re wearing your vigilante identity openly, then it doesn’t particularly matter whether you can transition between social and vigilante quickly, as you’ll just use social most of the time, even in combat.




13th Level


Immediate Change (Ex): Although immediate change isn’t quite as do-or-die mandatory as quick change, it’s yet another layer of protection against getting caught off guard in your social identity when combat breaks out. A move action is pretty low on action costs, all things considered. Similar calculus as quick change for figuring out whether you need it or not.
