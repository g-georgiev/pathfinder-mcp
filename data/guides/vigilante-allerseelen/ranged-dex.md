# Ranged (DEX)

Ranged (DEX)


        Holy cannoli, did someone at Paizo forget to send a few vigilante talents to the printer!? For a class that prides itself on being able to facilitate nearly any combat build under the sun, vigilantes get almost zero support for ranged builds. I mean, there’s the tragic gunmaster and warlock archetypes, if you want to roleplay a person who stinks at everything, and some minor support for thrown weapon builds through the teisatsu archetype and returning weapon vigilante talent. If you want to be a boilerplate archer, though? Zilch, zip, nada. Even the volatile arrows vigilante talent, while powerful, is dramatically limited by its uses per day. Go be a zen archer monk, pistolero or bolt ace gunslinger, hinterlander prestige class, or base slayer if you want archery so badly.
