# Renown Line

Renown Line


1st Level


Safe House (Ex): The safe house talent is a fantastic way to hide yourself and your group from prying eyes, magical or mundane. And besides, doesn’t everyone secretly want their own Batcave?


Renown (Ex): Renown is the starting point for the largest social talent web in the vigilante class, but to be honest, the talent isn’t that great on its own. You get some benefits to social interaction skills and starting attitudes from some NPCs, but the size of settlement you can affect with the talent is painfully small. What’s more, the trade-off between the amount of time you need to spend cultivating renown and the benefits you accrue for doing so simply doesn’t make sense unless you’re adventuring in one metropolis for a significant portion of a book in a Paizo AP.


Discreet Inquiries (Ex): Although I appreciate the flavor behind discreet inquiries, it guards against an eventuality that’s vanishingly rare. Just make your Diplomacy checks to gather information and damn the torpedos.
Obscurity (Ex): Unless you traded away seamless guise, that class feature and its +20 bonus to Disguise should keep you safe enough from scrutiny. Don’t do anything stupid like jump into combat in your social identity and you’ll be fine without obscurity.
