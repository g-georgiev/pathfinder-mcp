# Selected Other Races

Selected Other Races


        Under normal circumstances, I do a whole write-up of non-core races that can work for a class. But if I’m being honest, the vigilante doesn’t have a large enough stable of good favored class bonuses to make one non-core race demonstrably better than another. If you have bonuses to Charisma or Strength/Dexterity and no penalties to Constitution, you’re probably going to do just fine, attribute-wise. Everything else is about finding a theme and a few mechanical perks you like. So go, then: there are other sections than this.
