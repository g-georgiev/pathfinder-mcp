Strategy


I wanted a natural attack build that used the Chu Ye enforcer archetype, and I got my wish! Once you understand how the archetype’s polymorph abilities fit together, this build is actually pretty straightforward: you’ll generally start off with a Dazzling Display to demoralize enemies, close with the one you want to beat up, then start full-attacking. You’ll have your flurry of blows made as kicks, followed by four natural attacks (claw, claw, bite, horns) from Chu Ye enforcer. If even one attack hits, the enemy becomes flat-footed against all the rest of your attacks, allowing you to trigger your hidden strike damage repeatedly; I’d recommend cruel handwraps and armor with the malevolent enchantment to take advantage of all the nastiness that demoralization can leverage. By 15th level, you’ll be able to assume giant form I more or less at will during combat, and will have Signature Skill: Intimidate, Disheartening Display, and Multiattack to further amp up the pain on enemies. Not particularly nuanced as a build, but undeniably effective.
