# Variant Multiclassing

Variant Multiclassing


        Variant multiclassing can be a good option for classes with many bonus feats to spare, usually full martial classes looking to add some versatility, spellcasting, etc. Vigilantes exist in a strange space where they have lots of vigilante talents that grant feats (strike the unseen, shield of blades, vigilante’s reflexes, vital punishment, favored maneuver, etc.) but may not be the exact right feats you need to progress down a feat line.
