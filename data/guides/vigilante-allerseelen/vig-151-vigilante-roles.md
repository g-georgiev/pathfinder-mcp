# VIG 151: Vigilante Roles

VIG 151: Vigilante Roles


        It’s become customary in my guides for me to lay out the roles—both in-combat and out-of-combat—that Pathfinder classes can become great at. The vigilante is above all a modular class: everyone receives certain basics like startling appearance, dual identity, and seamless guise, but beyond that, the class (whether for a rogue-like stalker or a fighter-like avenger) is whatever you decide you want it to be. And that’s pretty cool! I can come up with a build for everything from a silent, shuriken-slinging ninja to a raging behemoth wielding a greatsword with equal ease in this class. Ya just gotta know whatcha want.
