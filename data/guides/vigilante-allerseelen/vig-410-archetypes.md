# VIG 410: Archetypes

VIG 410: Archetypes


Whereas most classes have a base class and some number of archetypes, vigilantes are unique in that they already come loaded with a pseudo-archetype—that is, the distinction between avenger and stalker. Some archetypes will lock you into one specialization or another (teisatsu are always stalkers, e.g.), some give you the choice, and some replace your specialization entirely with other class features. It’s…a bit of a cognitive mess, if I’m honest. But that’s what this guide is here for! Let me gently guide your inflatable giraffe down the lazy river of vigilante options. As usual in my guides, I’ve split the archetypes into ones that generally strengthen the class (the wheat) and ones that generally weaken the class (the chaff). You’re welcome to go against the grain (no pun intended) if you like, but just know that your work building an effective combatant might be a smidge harder in the chaff archetypes.
