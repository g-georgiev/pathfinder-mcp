# VIG 660: Magic Items

VIG 660: Magic Items


        I used to go into much greater depth on wondrous items, unique weapons, unique armors, etc. in my guides, but that all changed when I finished The Armamentarium. Simply put, this is a set of three documents for each of the types of magical item listed above. In addition to giving a mechanics-only shorthand with consistent formatting for every item in the game (well, slotted item, anyway) The Armamentarium also adds tags to each item so that you can filter it by use case. Some tags that vigilantes are particularly interested in:


      * [Vigilante]: Duh.
      * [Charisma], [Strength], [Dexterity]: Your main attack and save DC stats should need no explanation.
      * [Cleave], [Crit], [Finesse], [Improvised], [Melee], [Mounted], [Natural], [Nonlethal], [Ranged], [Reach], [Toxin], [TWF], [Unarmed]: Since there’s no such thing as a “standard” vigilante combat build, it seems reasonable to throw just about every tag into the mix here.
      * [ResDivination], [Anticaster]: Remember how annoying spellcasters are at figuring out that you’re not what you appear to be? Items with these tags will help alleviate that annoyance.
      * [Face], [Interrogation], [Bluff], [Diplomacy], [Intimidate], [Sense Motive]: Great tags for anyone who needs to deal with social interaction regularly. You have a Charisma focus and tons of social talents that lend themselves to this role, so prepare for that “anyone” to be you.
      * [Infiltration], [Scouting], [Mobility], [Acrobatics], [Disable Device], [Escape Artist], [Perception], [Stealth]: For moving around unseen or getting into places you’re not supposed to be.
      * [Intrigue], [Disguise], [Sleight of Hand]: For all-around ways to mess with enemies’ minds and spring clever traps.
      * [Ambush]: A key tag for stalker vigilantes, since it usually deals with how to get sneak attack or hidden strike damage more effectively.
      * [Disease], [Poison], [Tank], [Fortitude]: Generally good defensive tags for the vigilante, with their weak Fortitude save.
      * [Feint], [Grapple], [Steal], etc.: Many vigilantes go in for combat maneuver builds; these are your tags.
