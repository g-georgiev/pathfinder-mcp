# Wildsoul

Wildsoul


The Basics


        Even more so than avenging beasts, wildsouls are in tune with the natural world, emulating aspects and abilities of one particular type of animal. This archetype is really four archetypes in one, so it’s best to read carefully.


        Versatility: Varies

The Abilities


        (1) Natural Course (Ex): Alters vigilante specialization and replaces 2nd-, 6th-, 12th-, and 16th-level vigilante talents. So, the first thing to get out of the way with the wildsoul is that they’re still stalkers or avengers: no matter which natural course you pick, you can still be either a full-BAB mauler or a ¾-BAB hidden strike user. Either way, it’s worth thinking about whether your animal form will grant you vigilante talents from one specialization or another, as it may predispose you to take the opposite specialization in order to broaden your horizons.
